After mentioning my role and responsibility i have not mention WHAT EXACTLY IS ABU Robocon? ABU Robocon, or the Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union Robot Contest, is an annual robotics competition for university, college, and polytechnic students in the Asia-Pacific region. Participants design and build robots to compete in various tasks based on a specific theme or game scenario set each year. The competition promotes creativity, teamwork, and technical skills among students, fostering innovation and engineering excellence in the field of robotics.
